# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to act professionally and respectfully.

Examples of expected behavior:

- give technical feedback without personal attacks;
- assume good faith until there is evidence otherwise;
- keep reviews focused on code, product, and safety;
- respect legal, licensing, and security boundaries.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation;
- discriminatory language or conduct;
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission;
- knowingly submitting malicious code or deceptive changes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or ban participants for behavior that harms the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer or repository owner.
