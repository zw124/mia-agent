# Contributor Ladder

## Community contributor

- opens issues;
- submits small pull requests;
- agrees to the CLA and repository standards.

## Trusted contributor

- submits recurring, high-quality changes;
- demonstrates good review hygiene;
- may be invited into deeper roadmap discussions.

## Maintainer

- reviews and merges changes;
- enforces security and licensing policy;
- manages release, roadmap, and contribution boundaries.

## Commercial collaborator

- works under separate commercial terms where needed;
- may access private support, roadmap, or deployment workstreams not handled in the public repo.
